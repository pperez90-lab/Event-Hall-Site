About Soulstice Music Hall

A destination for inspiration, experience, and culture in Porto

Our History

Soulstice Music Hall opened its doors in 2002, quickly becoming Porto's most sought-after venue for unforgettable live events. With a passion for community, we've grown from humble jazz roots to an international hotspot hosting artists from all over the world.

  • Founded in Porto in 2002
  • Hosted first city-wide Jazz Festival in 2004
  • Added 400-seat outdoor space in 2012
  • Latest renovation completed 2025

Our Mission

To foster meaningful connections and creativity through world-class music, cultural events, and outstanding hospitality. We believe in the power of live experience to unite and inspire.
